About Us

AgroKings seeks to address issues on agricultural development in Africa using one of its developed products - AgriCoin. Through AgriCoin, we bring to the fore financial support and modern technology to aid farmers in their day-to-day farming activities. We also provide information on various market support services available to increase productivity, incomes and livelihoods of farmers.
