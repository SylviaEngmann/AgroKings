Our Products

AgriCoin

Looking to expand but run out of funds? AgriCoin is your single investment outfit that increases your output

Ploughing

The farmer to tractor ratio is one of the major challenges confronting farmers in Africa. This leads to the late planting of crops; we provide tractor services to smallholder farmers to help them cultivate a larger area for higher yield.

Harvesting To Market (H2M)

We harvest crops for smallholder farmers so that the produce will be in good shape for marketing. This also helps to prevent losses during harvesting.

Marketing

Marketing of Agricultural produce is sometimes challenging to most smallholders. AgroKings provides ready market for produce. Farmers therefore do not struggle to sell their produce.
